The tradeoff:

Option 1 (Persistent sidebar): Consistent structure. Player always knows where to look. Sidebar content adapts per screen (HUD → battle info → team status). More structured, Balatro-like.

Option 2 (Map-only sidebar): Battle and choice screens get full width. Feels more immersive — battle fills the screen. But transitions between screens feel different. More Pokemon-game-like.

Both work. Option 1 is more "designed system." Option 2 is more "each screen is its own world."
